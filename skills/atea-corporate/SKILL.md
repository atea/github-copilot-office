# Atea Corporate Visual Identity

When creating or styling PowerPoint slides, follow Atea's corporate visual identity guidelines strictly. Atea is a Nordic IT infrastructure company. The brand is clean, professional, credible, and vibrant.

## Brand Name Rules

- Always write "Atea" with capital A and lowercase tea — never "ATEA" (unless all surrounding text is uppercase or referring to the stock ticker).
- Tagline: "Specialists in Information Technology"
- Vision: "The Place to Be" (TP2B)
- Mission: Localized per country, e.g. "We Build the Future with IT" (for Atea ASA)
- Never mix the vision and mission in the same sentence.

## Logo Usage

The Atea logo files are available as SVG in the `references/` folder: `atea_logo_grey.svg` (primary) and `atea_logo_white.svg` (for dark/coloured backgrounds).

### Preferred Reproduction

- **Default:** Grey logo on white background (primary use).
- **On green or grey backgrounds:** Use the white logo.
- **On black or coloured backgrounds:** Use the white logo.
- **Black logo exception:** Only permitted in logo collections or on facades where all logos must be black due to design requirements.

### Logo on Images

- Place the logo on a single-colour background area or on images with a neutral/mid-tone background.
- Use simple, clean images with few elements so the logo remains legible.
- **Don't** place the logo on complicated, patterned, or low-contrast backgrounds.

### Logo Positioning

- When Atea is the main sender: logo in the **bottom-right corner** (footer).
- For internal use or letters: logo may be in the **top-left corner** (header).
- When Atea is not the main sender (e.g., partner ads): logo in the **bottom-left corner** (footer).
- **Never** use two Atea logos on the same page, slide, or banner.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colours (use these as default)

| Role              | Name            | Hex      | Usage                                       |
|-------------------|-----------------|----------|---------------------------------------------|
| Primary Green     | Atea Green      | 008A00   | Accent boxes, sender identity, chart fills   |
| Primary Grey      | Atea Grey       | 4D575D   | Logo color, headings, body text on light bg  |
| Light Grey        | Atea Light Grey | EDEEEE   | Subtle backgrounds, section fills            |
| White             | White           | FFFFFF   | Slide backgrounds, text on dark/green bg     |

- Atea Green (`008A00`) and Atea Grey (`4D575D`) are WCAG 2 Level AA compliant for white text.
- Use shades/tints of green and grey for backgrounds and illustrations, but **never** for objects or banners with white text unless the full-strength primary color is used.

### Secondary Colours (use only for charts, illustrations — requires purpose)

| Hex      | Name / Usage               |
|----------|----------------------------|
| F6BD18   | Yellow — charts, accents   |
| EC7A2E   | Orange — charts, accents   |
| D62429   | Red — charts, warnings     |
| 097288   | Teal — charts, accents     |
| 483D7C   | Purple — charts, accents   |
| 0965B1   | Blue — charts, accents     |

- Secondary colours may be used in PowerPoint illustrations and charts.
- **Do not** use secondary colours as primary colours in ads, banners, or invitations.
- When using ≤3 colours, prefer the primary colours (green, grey, white).
- When using many colours (e.g., charts), use the secondary palette.

### Chart Color Rules

- For pie charts and bar charts, different colours may be combined. Gradients of a single colour are also acceptable.
- Preferred sequence for charts: `008A00`, `4D575D`, `F6BD18`, `EC7A2E`, `D62429`, `097288`, `483D7C`, `0965B1`
- The same rules apply to table graphs.

## Typography

### For PowerPoint (internal production)

- **Headlines and titles:** Arial Bold
- **Body text:** Arial Regular
- **Alternative body text (high-text):** Times New Roman
- These correspond to the digital fonts Inter and Crimson Pro.

### If Inter is available (digital/modern contexts)

- **Short headlines:** Inter Bold
- **Longer headlines:** Inter Light
- **Campaign one-word headlines:** Inter Black
- **Body text:** Inter Regular
- **Captions:** Inter Light
- Do **not** use green text for headlines.
- Do **not** use handwritten styles for headlines.

### Font Sizes (PowerPoint)

- **Title slides:** fontSize 36–44, bold, color `4D575D`
- **Section headers:** fontSize 28–32, bold, color `4D575D`
- **Slide headings:** fontSize 24–28, bold, color `4D575D`
- **Body text:** fontSize 16–18, color `4D575D`
- **Captions / footnotes:** fontSize 12–14, color `4D575D`

## Layout Principles

- **Slide background:** White (`FFFFFF`) is the default.
- **Format:** Always use 16:9 format with 10x5.625 inches
- **Title position:** x: 0.5, y: 0.4, w: 9, h: 0.9
- **Subtitle position:** x: 0.5, y: 1.3, w: 9, h: 0.6
- **Body content area:** x: 0.5, y: 2.0, w: 9, h: 4.5
- **Two-column layout:** Left column w: 4.2, right column x: 5.3, w: 4.2
- **Logo placement:** Bottom-right corner of the slide (Atea is the main sender). For internal use, top-left is also acceptable.
- **Logo negative space:** Maintain clear space around the logo equivalent to the height of the "A" in the logo.

## Shapes & Accents

- **Green accent boxes:** Use a filled rectangle with fill `008A00` and white text to reinforce Atea sender identity. This is a core visual element.
- **Dividers:** Thin horizontal line (h: 0.03) with fill `008A00` below headings.
- **Section background boxes:** Fill `EDEEEE` (Atea Light Grey) for subtle content grouping.
- **Callout boxes:** Fill `EDEEEE`, no border or border color `4D575D`.
- Do **not** use green text for headlines — green is for boxes and accents only.

## Tables

- Header row: fill `008A00`, color `FFFFFF`, bold (Arial Bold), fontSize 14
- Body rows: alternating fills `FFFFFF` and `EDEEEE`, color `4D575D`, fontSize 13
- Border: pt 1, color `EDEEEE`
- When ≤3 colours needed, stick to primary palette (green header, grey text, light grey alternating rows).

## Images & Visual Style

- Images must feel **authentic, credible, and vibrant** — not staged or contrived.
- Prefer images of people, active and positive scenes.
- Mix black-and-white images with colour pictures for variety.
- Avoid cluttered backgrounds, manipulated images, and postcard motifs.
- One message per image.
- If an image has contrast problems with green overlays, convert the image to greyscale.

## Green Box Pattern

A signature Atea visual element: a green rectangle (`008A00`) with white text placed on or adjacent to an image or as a standalone callout. Use this for:
- Key statistics or quotes
- Section callouts
- Vision/mission statements (e.g., "We Build the Future with IT" in Inter Bold)

## Vision & Mission on Slides

- **TP2B (The Place to Be):** Use the official TP2B logo if available. Write "TP2B" in uppercase.
- **Mission text:** Write in Inter Bold (or Arial Bold), tracking tight, one or two lines. A green box behind the text is acceptable.
- Never create custom logos for the vision or mission — use text only for the mission.
- Never combine vision and mission in the same text block.

## Do's and Don'ts

- ✅ Use white slide backgrounds as default
- ✅ Use Atea Green (`008A00`) for accent boxes and chart fills
- ✅ Use Atea Grey (`4D575D`) for all text
- ✅ Keep generous white space — clean Nordic aesthetic
- ✅ Use green boxes with white text as a signature visual element
- ✅ Use secondary colours only in charts and illustrations
- ❌ Don't use green for headline text — only for filled shapes/boxes
- ❌ Don't use secondary colours as the primary palette
- ❌ Don't alter or recolor the Atea logo
- ❌ Don't mix vision ("The Place to Be") and mission ("We Build the Future with IT") together
- ❌ Don't use the old logo with the green E
- ❌ Don't place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
- ❌ Don't use font sizes below 12pt
